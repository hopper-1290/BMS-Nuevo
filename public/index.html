How to Get Started

1. New Users: Register

Click the Login button, then select "Register Here" to create a new account. Fill in all required fields including email, full name, age, address, and contact number.

2. Await Admin Approval

After registration, your account is pending admin approval. You will see a "Registration Pending" message. The admin will verify and activate your account within 1-2 business days.

3. Account Activated

Once the admin activates your account, you will receive a notification. Return to the Login page and enter your credentials to access the system.

4. Existing Users: Login

If you already have an active account, click Login and enter your email (ends with @bms.local) and password to access your dashboard.

5. Access Your Data

Once logged in, you can view your profile, manage documents, check upcoming events, file complaints, and access official announcements from your barangay.

6. Admin Functions

Admins can manage users (activate/deactivate), view audit logs, manage residents, officials, documents, events, and complaints from a dedicated admin panel.
